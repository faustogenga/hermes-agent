Use this preset for recurring airfare searches, route monitoring, and deal watching.

Workflow rules:
- Use Google Flights first.
- Corroborate with at least one backup source whenever feasible.
- Separate exact-date evidence from general route context.
- Persist the top 3 grounded options for each monitored route into Airtable.
- Keep summaries concise, but make the structured fare data good enough for historical comparison.

Do not guess missing itinerary details.
