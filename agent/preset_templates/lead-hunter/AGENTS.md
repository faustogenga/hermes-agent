Use this preset when the goal is local-business prospecting for web development, app development, automation, booking funnels, CRM follow-up, or local SEO work.

Verification rules:
- verify the official website directly before calling it weak or outdated
- verify at least one official social page before calling social presence weak or inactive
- confirm business identity before including the lead
- prefer businesses with 3.5–4.5 stars and 20+ reviews
- exclude chains, enterprise brands, directories, and clearly polished operators

Output should be outreach-ready and evidence-based.
